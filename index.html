Our Mission

Educo’s mission is to provide free, high-quality academic support and meaningful service opportunities to students across Georgia. Through peer and classroom tutoring in subjects like Algebra, Chemistry, English, and SAT prep, we empower students to help each other learn and grow. Beyond academics, we foster a spirit of service—organizing book and school supply drives, awarding over 800 accredited service hours, and hosting monthly community events to uplift local organizations and schools.

Our Vision

We envision a statewide network of student-led Educo chapters transforming education through tutoring and service. By equipping students with the tools to support one another, we aim to create a culture of leadership, compassion, and academic equity—starting in Forsyth County and expanding across Georgia.
